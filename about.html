Tips & Tricks (Cheat Sheet)

  • Focus on remembering colors by their position rather than trying to memorize all at once.
  • Start matching from the corners - they're easier to remember!
  • If you're stuck, try to remember just one color pair at a time.
  • The game gets easier with practice - play a few rounds to get the hang of it!
