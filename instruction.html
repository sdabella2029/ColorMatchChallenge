How to Play

Step 1

Click "Start Game" to begin. The timer will start counting down from 60 seconds.

Step 2

Memorize the colors shown in the grid. You have 3 seconds to study them.

Step 3

After colors hide, click on tiles to reveal them. Match pairs with the same color.

Step 4

Match all pairs before time runs out! Each match gives you 10 points.
